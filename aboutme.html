About me

Dr. (Mrs.) Manjusha Sapkal is practising Ayurveda and Panchkarma for last 30 years. She completed her B.A.M.S. from Nagpur University in the year 1987. Later in 1999 she persued a Diploma in Yoga and Naturopathy from Nagpur University (course affiliated with Delhi University). She secured First merit position in that Diploma course. Since then she has been in the service of the society to heal the patients and spread wellness.
